Hi there, my name is Joshua Kindla and I'm a product designer and producer based in San Antonio, Texas. One of my biggest passions is helping other people improve their lives through good design. I love learning new things and working with people who are passionate about what they do.

My favorite part about the design process is research. I am facinated with how people interact and consume day-to-day products, and am always thinking about how it can be improved. I would consider myself a big picture thinker, and love working on products from end to end.

Besides working on products, I also love to apply my design thinking to sound. Having created my own production company, I am fortunate to work with other artists and bring their visions to life.
